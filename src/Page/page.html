About SmartCalc_V2.0

This manual describes how to use the calculator SmartCalc_V1.0. This calculator includes: a classic engineering calculator with a charting function, a loan calculator and a deposit calculator. The calculator supports the following arithmetic operations and mathematical functions:

Evaluation of expressions with brackets (a + b);
Addition a + b;
Subtraction a - b;
Multiplication a * b;
Division a / b;
Exponentiation a^b;
Remainder of division a mod b;
Cosine calculation cos(x);
Calculating the sine sin(x);
Calculating the tangent tan(x);
Calculating the arc cosine acos(x);
Calculating the arcsine asin(x);
Calculating the arc tangent of atan(x);
Calculating the square root of sqrt(x);
Calculating the natural logarithm of ln(x);
Calculating the Decimal Logarithm of log(x);

How to work with engineer calculator

1. Enter the expression by clicking on the buttons

2. Press '=' to calculate the expression

3. Done

How to work with engineer calculator (Graph)

1. To open the graph section press 'Open Graph'

2. Click on the 'x' button to enter a value

3. After entering the value press 'done'

4. Enter an expression and click the 'Graph' button

5. Done

How to work with credit calculator

1. In the menu select switch to 'credit calculator'

2. Fill in the required fields with data
